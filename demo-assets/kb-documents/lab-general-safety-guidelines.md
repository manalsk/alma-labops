# Laboratory General Safety and Operational Guidelines

## Visibility
all_lab_members

## Purpose
This document provides general laboratory safety and operational guidelines for all members using ALMA LabOps.

## Basic Laboratory Rules
1. Wear appropriate PPE at all times:
   - Lab coat
   - Gloves
   - Eye protection when necessary
2. Do not eat or drink inside the laboratory.
3. Label all samples and containers clearly.
4. Report spills or accidents immediately.
5. Keep workspaces clean and organized.

## Inventory Rules
- Always check inventory before opening a new box.
- Update inventory quantities if you consume the last item.
- Do not move inventory items without updating storage location information.

## Package Intake
When receiving a package:
1. Inspect the package for visible damage.
2. Take a clear photo of the label.
3. Upload the package image into ALMA LabOps.
4. Verify extracted information before creating inventory items.

## Waste Disposal
- Biohazard waste goes into designated biohazard containers.
- Sharps must be placed in sharps containers only.
- Chemical waste must follow institutional disposal policies.

## Task Management
- Complete assigned tasks promptly.
- Update task status in ALMA LabOps.
- Notify a researcher if a task cannot be completed safely.

## Emergency Procedure
In case of emergency:
- Stop active experiments if safe to do so.
- Notify nearby personnel.
- Contact the PI or safety officer immediately.

## Notes
These guidelines apply to all students, researchers, and operational staff.
