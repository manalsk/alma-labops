# Restricted Procurement and Budget Policy

## Visibility
researchers_only

## Purpose
This document describes internal laboratory procurement budgeting and purchasing authority policies.

## Budget Authority
Only the Principal Investigator (PI) has authority to approve purchases using grant funds.

Researchers may:
- create purchase requests
- compare vendors
- recommend vendors
- estimate pricing

Researchers may not:
- directly approve spending
- disclose grant balances externally

## Internal Budget Guidelines
Typical annual operational allocation:
- Consumables: 40%
- Reagents: 35%
- Equipment maintenance: 15%
- Emergency reserve: 10%

## Preferred Vendors
Preferred vendors include:
- Thermo Fisher Scientific
- Sigma-Aldrich
- VWR
- Fisher Scientific

## Restricted Information
Students should not have access to:
- grant allocation details
- vendor pricing negotiations
- procurement spending summaries
- pending financial approvals

## Duplicate Procurement Prevention
Before approving purchases:
1. Check current inventory
2. Check pending procurement requests
3. Verify another lab member has not already ordered the item

## Notes
High-cost items above internal thresholds require PI review and justification.
